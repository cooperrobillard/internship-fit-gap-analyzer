Sample AI Engineering Internship

We are looking for an intern to support AI and software development projects.

Responsibilities:
- Build Python tools for internal workflows.
- Work with REST APIs and JSON data.
- Help analyze data and summarize recurring patterns.
- Support prototype development for AI-assisted applications.
- Document technical decisions and limitations.

Qualifications:
- Experience with Python.
- Familiarity with SQL, pandas, or data cleaning.
- Interest in machine learning and large language models.
- Familiarity with Git and GitHub.
- Strong communication and documentation skills.

Preferred:
- Experience with FastAPI.
- Exposure to LangChain or retrieval-augmented generation.
- Interest in responsible AI, evaluation, and human review.
